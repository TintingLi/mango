Mango

A bias correcting ChIA-PET analysis pipeline

What is Mango?

Mango is a complete ChIA-PET data analysis pipeline that provides statistical confidence estimates for DNA-DNA interactions and corrects for major sources of bias including differential peak enrichment and genomic proximity.
